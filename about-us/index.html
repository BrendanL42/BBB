Our Story

Hello!
Welcome to Beauty by Brendan.

For the majority of my life I was crippled with insecurity due to stretch marks that came up all over my body since young adolescence. This affected my life dramatically causing me to avoid beaches, pools or anywhere the people might see them and also affected my intimate relationships.
I have been endlessly searching for a solution that didn’t involve a major invasive procedure and have thrown a lot of money at the issue over the years with only minimal and temporary results.
Then it all changed for me when I found Fibroblast Skin Tightening. I had my first treatment which reduced my stretch marks dramatically. Finally, I could start to feel comfortable in my own skin and thus Beauty by Brendan was born. Being so thrilled with my results and a new found confidence I felt this burning desire to share this incredible treatment with others.
Since then I have been able to assist people with all different kinds of things, it’s been an incredible journey and I’ve found my true calling bringing confidence to people through Fibroblast Skin Tightening.

Get in touch, we’re happy to answer any questions and will always offer judgement free & honest advice.
Look forward to hearing from you,

Brendan.
