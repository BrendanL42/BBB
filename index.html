What is Plasma Fibroblast

Fibroblast Plasma Skin Tightening Treatment – a new game-changing technique in the industry that will allow you to provide a non-surgical, non-invasive alternative treatment to skin tightening that suits almost everyone and can treat several different areas. This treatment works by using a hand-held device (Plasma pen) that ionizes gasses in the air between its tip (the probe) and the skin, creating a small and localised plasma charge which is used to treat the area of the skin selected for plasma skin tightening. The small plasma charge causes superficial damage to the upper dermal layer of the skin and transmits heat to the lower layers, which reactivates the collagen production, tightening and rejuvenating the skin leaning behind some incredible results.
